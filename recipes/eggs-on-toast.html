Eggs on toast

Feta cheese is the secret ingredient that takes scrambled eggs from good to great! With this recipe, worry not about untasty eggs on your toast.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh dill or parley to garnish

Steps

  1. Spread thin layer of butter on both sides of the bread.
  2. Fry bread over medium heat for about 2 minutes on each side.
  3. Remove bread and set aside.
  4. Add 1/2 tbsp of butter to pan.
  5. Add eggs and feta cheese to pan and cook, stirring often, for about 3-4 minuites.
  6. Divide eggs on the bread slices and sprinke them with freshly chopped dill or parsley.
  7. Enjoy!
