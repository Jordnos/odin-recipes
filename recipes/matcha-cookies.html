Matcha Cookies

Matcha and white chocolate chips! Thats the secret.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (plain flour)
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp matha green tea powder
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 pinch Diamond crystal kosher salt
  • 130g confectioners' sugar (1 cup + 2 tsp)
  • 2 large egg yolks (room temp)
  • 1.4 cup White chocolate chips (or macadamia nuts)

Steps

  1. Combine flour and matcha green tea powder and sift.
  2. Beat unsalted butter until creamy.
  3. Add salt to butter and blend.
  4. Add confectioners' sugar and beat until soft and light.
  5. Add 2 large egg yolks and mix until well combined.
  6. Gradually add flour/matcha mix until just combined.
  7. Add white chocolate chips and mix until just incorporated.
  8. Divide dough in 2 and shape each piece into cylinder shapes. (1 1/2 inches (4 cm) in diameter and 7 inches (18 cm) long)
  9. Refrigerate until firm (at least 2 hours).
  10. Preheat oven to 350F.
  11. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking liner.
  12. Cut dough into 1/3 inches (7mm) thick pieces.
  13. Place on baking sheet with around 1 inch (2.5 cm) between them.
  14. Bake for 15 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  15. Enjoy! (Can store them for at least 4 days in airtight container)
